BACKGROUND

As a family owned business,UMAMA has become much loved in the homes of many Pretoria residents.
For some we have catered all the way through from their 50th birthdays to the kids sweet 16, graduations and even weddings!
This is the essence of what we stand for. We deliver a personalised service, custom designed to make you happy.
UMAMA have travelled South Africa, cooked to some of the influential greats across the country, opened a few restaurants in Cape Town and finally decided to go back to their roots in Pretoria.

HISTORY

UMAMA FOODS was founded by Keanna Taaibosch in 2012. After interning for one of the leading coordinators in South Africa she saw a market for bringing out a bespoeke events business to enhance individual’s and corporate’s creativity and uniqueness without blowing budgets in doing this.
She studied a BCOM Marketing Management degree at the University of Johannesburg as well as obtaining an Intec Diploma for Events Management and an Intec Certificate for Wedding Planning. She prides herself in keeping up to date in events trends and an always learning attitude.
