FOOD CATERGORIES

Drinks

Our drinks are categorized as alcoholic or non-alcoholic. Major alcoholic categories include fermented beverages like beer and wine, and distilled spirits such as whiskey and vodka.
Non-alcoholic categories are diverse, encompassing water, juices, sodas, teas, coffees, and dairy-based drinks. We classified our beverages: Fruit beverages. Fruit beverages are one of the popular categories of beverages that are easily digestible, highly refreshing, thirst quenching, appetizing and nutritionally superior. *Vegetable beverages. *Milk based beverages. *Carbonated beverages. *Malted beverages. *Tea and Coffee.

Salads

With everything from green salads to pasta salads and more, We have broken down our range of Salads into four main categories–tossed, composed, bound, and those without lettuce (this is our own personal addition, because we believe pasta and grain salads are very much worthy of being in the salad family)

Main Course

we provide exquisite catering and design services to create a unique and memorable experience for you and your guests. We take a modern approach, creating customized menus made from the finest local ingredients to please a variety of tastes and styles. And we don’t just stop at the food!
We serve an appetizer, soup, entrée, dessert, and cheese. A six-course meal offers hors-d'oeuvres, soup, fish, and an entrée, followed by salad, coffee, and dessert.

Dessert

Our selection of desserts can be served individually plated as part of a set menu or may be ordered for you to serve at your private function.
Our dessert categories include cakes and pastries, cookies and bars, frozen desserts (like ice cream and gelato), puddings and custards, fruit-based desserts (like pies and cobblers), candies, and gelatin desserts, with other categories like deep-fried, chocolate, and dessert drinks.

Weddings
